FAQ'S

What is an FAQ Page?

Our FAQ page answers the most frequently asked questions you may be questioning about our program. Bellxcell want to ensure that we cover everything we are about and what we offer. If there is a question you have that is not listed in our FAQ page, feel free to contact us.

What is the benefit for my program to have a platform?

The benefit for your program to be on our platform is that your students will be able to access your resources during and after your program. You may have a lot of information for a student to remember or there may be too many papers that are being handed out that my be lost. Putting your program on our platform will bring all of this information into one place and set in the way that you like for student to view and access this information.

How much does it cost?

The price varies based on what features your program wants on our platform. Please fill out the Contact Us form so that we can continue our conversation. We look forward to working with you!

I don't have a program but I want to support, how?

There are a lot of people who support our platform but don't have a program. For our supporters, you can Donate to our non-profit to support other programs get the platform they need for students to easily access their resources. Every donation bridges the gap for education for other students needing an education, role model, and support.
